Our Mission


EXCEPTIONAL CARS, EXCEPTIONAL SERVICE

Our mission, quite simply is to be the best prestige car hire company in the business. We take pride in every aspect of our business, from the minute we pick up the phone to the day we deliver your car.

We have a highly skilled customer service department who know all our cars inside out. This means that when you book a car with us you can be confident that you will know exactly all the car has to offer in terms of specification, colour and interior finish ensuring you get the right car for your hire experience. We believe service is the most important parts of our business, so if you want a car we do not stock, we will endeavor to find the car you do want.

Ace Car aim to handover only truly exceptional vehicles to our clients. To that end we employ a dedicated valet team who are trained to maintain each vehicle to a ‘showroom plus’ standard. Vehicles are never released without a through interior and exterior examination, full preparation and mechanical checks.
